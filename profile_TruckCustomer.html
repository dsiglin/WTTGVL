About

With every bite of delicious roadkill, Nick's Roadkill Delight will delight you with it's slightly asphalt taste. Sure some other food trucks promise you fresher food but nothing comes close to that slow aged, sun baked goodness that only Nick's serves. You can always count on us to deliver the most unique and . . . interesting meal combinations.
